Welcome to Dragon Repeller

In the realm of Eldoria, Arvindor city suffers beneath the rule of Igniscale, an ancient dragon guarding a powerful relic. Fear grips the villagers as they long for liberation. You, a destined hero known as the Dragon Repeller, answer the call. With bravery as your guide, you venture forth to free the city, confront Igniscale, and restore Arvindor's lost glory. Armed with courage and determination, your journey unfolds, intertwining your destiny with the city's as you prepare for an epic battle to bring peace and freedom back to Arvindor.
